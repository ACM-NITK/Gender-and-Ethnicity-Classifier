For this classification 
1. Reading dataset ,shuffling and coverting ethnicity classes to categories is done
2. Images are converted to vectors and normalized
3. dataset is divided into train,validation and test.
4. CNN model with CONV-POOL-CONV-POOL-FC architecture along with RELu activation and dropout
5. Using Adam optimization model is trained
6. Training Accuracy -87% , Validation accuracy-80% and test accuracy -80% is obtained
